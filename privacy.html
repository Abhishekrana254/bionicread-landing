Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What data we collect

BionicRead processes webpage text locally in your browser to apply bionic formatting. We do not read, store, or transmit the content of pages you visit.

We use Google Analytics on bionicread.vercel.app (page views, button clicks, demographics where available) and anonymous extension events (install, popup open, bionic toggle) when analytics is configured. No page content is collected. Payment email is stored only when you purchase Pro.

Chrome extension permissions

storage: Save your settings and daily usage count.
activeTab: Apply bionic mode on the current tab when you toggle.
host_permissions (<all_urls>): Required to transform text on pages you choose to read.

Third parties

Payments processed by Razorpay. Analytics may use Google Analytics on the landing page. We do not sell your data.

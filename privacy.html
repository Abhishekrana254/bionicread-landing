BionicRead Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

BionicRead is a Chrome browser extension developed by Abhishek Rana (abhishekrana254@gmail.com).

What we collect

Nothing about the pages you read. All bionic text formatting runs locally in your browser. We do not read, store, or transmit webpage content.

Settings you choose (such as intensity preferences) may be stored locally in Chrome's extension storage on your device only.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We do not track browsing history on our servers
  • We do not collect personal information unless you email us for support

Third parties

The extension is distributed via the Chrome Web Store, which is governed by Google's policies.
